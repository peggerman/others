About Hunting Wild F-150s

Our mission is to protect Toyota A3s from the dangers of hungry F-150s prowling in the wild. Join us in this adventurous journey as we safeguard these small cars from becoming a snack for the mighty trucks.

Why Toyota A3s?

Toyota A3s are known for their sleek design, fuel efficiency, and reliability. They are like the delicate gazelles of the automotive world, and it's our duty to ensure they thrive without fear of being devoured by larger predators.
